Our Story

Where Nature Meets
Master Craftsmanship

Tuscany Stone Boutique was founded on one principle: every great space deserves materials that carry a story. We bridge the world's most exceptional quarries with architects, interior designers, and homeowners across Indonesia.

Our curated portfolio of over 850 natural and engineered stone products spans marble, granite, onyx, travertine, limestone, quartzite, and rare semi-precious materials — each rigorously selected for character, durability, and authenticity.

Natural Stone

Featured
Marble Collection
Polished Honed Fluted Textured

Premium marbles with unique veining and rich colour variations — Calacatta, Statuario, Carrara, and more. Timeless elegance for countertops, flooring, wall cladding, and decorative elements in both classic and contemporary interiors.

Best for: Countertops · Flooring · Feature Walls · Bathrooms
View Collection Request Sample
Granite Collection
Polished Flamed Brushed

One of the hardest natural stones available, granite offers unmatched durability and a dramatic crystalline aesthetic. Ideal for high-traffic surfaces that need to perform as beautifully as they look.

Best for: Kitchen Countertops · Exterior Cladding · Commercial Flooring
View Collection Request Sample
Onyx Collection
Backlit Translucent Polished

Rare and dramatic, onyx is prized for its translucency and intense banded colouration. When backlit, it transforms into a glowing statement — making it the stone of choice for luxury feature walls, reception desks, and bar fronts.

Best for: Feature Walls · Bar Fronts · Reception Desks · Luxury Interiors
View Collection Request Sample
Travertine Collection
Filled & Honed Tumbled Cross-cut

Warm, textured, and deeply organic — travertine's natural pores and tonal variation give spaces an earthy sophistication. A staple of Mediterranean architecture and increasingly popular in resort and spa environments.

Best for: Bathroom Tiles · Pool Surrounds · Wall Cladding · Spa Interiors
View Collection Request Sample
Quartzite Collection
Polished Leathered Brushed

Stronger than marble and more visually striking than granite, quartzite offers the best of both worlds. Its sweeping patterns and silica-rich composition make it a premium choice for large-format slabs and statement surfaces.

Best for: Kitchen Islands · Large-Format Flooring · Wall Panels
View Collection Request Sample
Limestone Collection
Honed Sandblasted Brushed

Soft, muted, and endlessly versatile, limestone brings a quiet sophistication to any interior. Its consistent tone makes it ideal for minimal, refined spaces where the architecture does the talking.

Best for: Hotel Lobbies · Flooring · Exterior Paving · Feature Walls
View Collection Request Sample
Sandstone Collection
Natural Split Sawn Brushed

With its warm, sun-baked tones and natural layered texture, sandstone evokes the landscape it came from. Durable and slip-resistant, it's especially suited to outdoor and tropical environments.

Best for: Outdoor Paving · Garden Walls · Tropical Villas · Pool Areas
View Collection Request Sample

25-Year Warranty
Dekton®
Ultra-Compact UV Resistant Zero Porosity Scratch Resistant

Dekton achieves in hours what nature takes thousands of years to create. Its patented TSP process transforms mineral particles into an ultra-compact porcelain surface with unmatched performance — scratch-proof, stain-proof, and UV-stable for indoor and outdoor use. Available in ultragloss, matte, and velvety textures with a 25-year warranty.

Best for: Kitchen Countertops · Outdoor Surfaces · Facades · High-Traffic Flooring
View Collection Request Sample
Silestone®
Quartz-based HybriQ+® Stain Resistant Hygienic

Silestone is a premium quartz surface engineered for beauty and endurance. Its HybriQ+® technology combines recycled quartz with hybrid mineral content, delivering surfaces that are non-porous, highly resistant to staining, and available in an extensive colour palette that mirrors natural stone.

Best for: Kitchen Countertops · Bathroom Vanities · Wall Cladding
View Collection Request Sample
SICIS Mosaic
Glass Mosaic Stone Mosaic Custom Pattern Art Installation

SICIS represents the highest expression of mosaic art — combining centuries of Venetian craft with contemporary design. From intricate geometric patterns to bespoke artistic murals, SICIS mosaic tiles transform walls, floors, and pools into works of art.

Best for: Pools · Feature Walls · Luxury Bathrooms · Art Installations

How It Works

From First Idea
to Finished Space

Our simple process makes sourcing premium stone effortless.

01

Consult
