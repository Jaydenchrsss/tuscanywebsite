Our Story

Where Nature Meets
Master Craftsmanship

Tuscany Stone Boutique was founded on one principle: every great space deserves materials that carry a story. We bridge the world's most exceptional quarries with architects, interior designers, and homeowners across Indonesia.

Our curated portfolio of over 850 natural and engineered stone products spans marble, granite, onyx, travertine, limestone, quartzite, and rare semi-precious materials — each rigorously selected for character, durability, and authenticity.

How It Works

From First Idea
to Finished Space

Our simple process makes sourcing premium stone effortless.

01

Consult

Share your project brief — our specialists help you identify the right material, finish, and format for your space.

02

Sample

Visit our Jakarta showroom or request samples delivered to you. See the stone, feel the texture, verify the colour in your light.

03

Fabricate

We cut, finish, and treat each slab to your exact specification — polished, honed, fluted, or custom profiled.

04

Deliver

On-time, site-ready delivery across Jakarta and nationwide. We coordinate directly with your contractor for a seamless install.
